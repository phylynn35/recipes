
Chewy Chocolate Macarrons

Ingredients:

- 1 pkg (14 oz) Bakers Angel Flake coconut
- 1 can (14 oz) Borden Eagle Brand Sweetened condensed milk (not evaporated milk) or can use a less sweetened condensed milk
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 4 squares Bakers unsweetened chocolate, melted


Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well.

3. Drop from a teaspons or a regular small spoon, 1 inch apart, on a well greased baking sheet.

4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from baking sheet at once and place on wax paper to cool. Makes about 8 dozen. 

5. The recipe can be halved.
